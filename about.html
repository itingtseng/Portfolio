👋 Hi, I'm Tiffany.

I'm an experienced product designer with a demonstrated history of working in the information technology and services industry. Currently, I'm pursuing a Masters in Human-Computer Interaction at Indiana University, Indianapolis.

Skilled in UI/UX design and project management, I'm a UX & Product Designer with a background in geographic information systems and data visualization for responsive websites and mobile applications.

My goal as a designer is to create a shared vision fostering shared values among all members of a product team. Outside of work, you can find me working out, photographing food, and watching Netflix from time to time. Additionally, I enjoy reading about psychology, design, technology, and discussing public issues.
