i-Environment

Web design

Abstract

Through the Open Platform for Environmental Resources, Taiwan EPA compiles real-time monitoring information to create i-Environment - a thematic interactive map browsing platform to serve demands of public for this type of information. An interface with dashboards and interactive maps provides location-based services that allow users to easily browse and search the environmental information they need. User can click the button of the dashboards to see the interaction on map.

  • Role

    UI/UX Designer

  • Technology

    CorelDRAW, HTML, Bootstrap, Local-based service, Google Maps API

  • Clients

    Environmental Protection Administration

  • Year

    May 2015-November 2015

Goal

Take the reference of MyEnvironment portal of the US Environmental Protection Agency and EU practices, building map query service portal which is integration, adaptability, theming and personalization for public demand. Based on user-centered design, i-Environment provides the environmental quality and life information according to user’s location.

Research

In line with open data policy, Environmental Protection Administration (EPA) continues to open up and promote public applications. "I-Environment" uses more than ten types of inter-departmental environmental open data in combination of location based service. Users can the environmental information effectively from searching location information. Through interaction map, user can also understand the surrounding environment. The personal environmental information map provides information based on the latest environmental activities from EPA and local Environmental Protection Agency.

Ideation

People from 8 to 80 years old could easily interact with i-Environment. It is an integrated, fast and convenience public service system. In order to enhance environmental awareness, environmental forecasting and warning information are provided so that user can take immediate precautions in daily life. i-Environment will integrate with the Environment Info Push App. User can use social media account to log in to access synced data of the app like my favorite location. They can also use social media sharing function and download services when not logged in.

Outcomes

i-Environment is the first government website designed as the Hybrid Web. It helps present reports, statistics, quantitative figures, and other environmental information in data visualization. An interface with dashboards and interactive maps provides location-based services that allow users to easily browse and search environmental information. In addition to the convenient information services, the sensor network also provides air quality alerts to the public, along with suggestions for appropriate activities which is reminding user of immediately responding to discomfort and risks from environmental pollution under various circumstances.
