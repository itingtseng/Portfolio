Environmental White Paper

Infographic

Abstract

The project's goal is to visualize the effect of air quality and water quality policies in recent years. The hardest part was fusion design and communication issues from numerous chaotic information sources, and the challenge of telling a story with limited time and budget. I discovered the breadth and profundity of data journalism and infographic during this time. This fascination convinced me to study and invest in information.

  • Roll

    User Interface Designer

  • Technology

    Coreldraw, HTML

  • Clients

    Environmental Protection Administration

  • Year

    2016

Goal

Based on publications such as the "Environmental White Paper" and "Taiwan Environmental Quality Report" published by the Environmental Protection Agency, process the trajectory of environmental protection work such as environmental policy and current conditions, environmental protection strategies and measures through infographic and GIS data. The follow-up provides the application of the map integration platform for the implementation of the Environmental Protection Agency, so that the public can better grasp the environmental protection and land and resources maintenance integration information.

Outcomes

  • Homepage

    TAt first, I planned to use parallax scrolling to tell a story. Then I decided to enhance the interaction effect of the webpage and animation. Finally, I settled on simplicity.

  • Navigation page

    The navigation page is the thorny and critical issue of the whole project. How do I let the public become aware of the improvement of environment but propagate policy simultaneously? This had puzzled me for a long time, but it became simple finally.

  • Interactive Charts

    There were 30 interactive charts in the website. I originally intended to design different charts for each data set separately, but this idea was unrealistic on the given timetable. Thus I prioritized consistency for the charts: interactive maps with interactive linecharts.
