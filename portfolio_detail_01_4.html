Environmental Protection Manifesto

Information Architecture

Abstract

In the era of opendata, to enhance public awareness of the environmental regulations and to understand the trends of monitoring data, use the data in Environmental Resources DataBase and consult the discipline policy in 1987-2014 Taiwan Environmental Quality Report and Environment White paper from 2003 to 2015. Throughout vectorization and digitization spatial data and series data, in combination with the related policy, people could realize the meaning of the environment policy. Depend on the data journalism, I choose the topic with the awarence of public issue, the collection of data, the visualization of data, and the sense of infographics, for giving web user a optimization user interface or experience.

  • Role

    User Interface Designer

  • Technology

    Coreldraw, HTML

  • Clients

    Environmental Protection Administration

  • Year

    2016

Goal

Based on publications such as the "Environmental Protection Manifesto" and "Taiwan Environmental Quality Report" published by the Environmental Protection Agency, process the trajectory of environmental protection work such as environmental policy and current conditions, environmental protection strategies and measures through infographic and GIS data. The follow-up provides the application of the map integration platform for the implementation of the Environmental Protection Agency, so that the public can better grasp the environmental protection and land and resources maintenance integration information.

Process

I. Data collection analysis and interview

In the data collection and interview phase, I conduct survey of relevant database, work process and requirements, application programming interface (API) process from each departmental data.

II. Data construction and system requirements analysis

After the investigation and analysis of the various data, I convert the results of each work demand analysis into a module structure diagram to facilitate the next planning and design stage.

III. System planning and design

After several working meeting discussion to determine the modules required for the system, detailed design will be carried out to describe the internal processing logic and the writing of the software program. In addition, interface also design during this process.

IV. System development

The main work is to develop the system interface environment and develop the features required by the system, and to implement the system analysis as the system features.

V. Evaluation and maintenance

System evaluations are carried out for the system to identify errors, perform maintenance and program corrections.

Outcomes

The Environmental Protection Manifesto project will serve as a digital learning environment. Through interactive maps and charts, the data will be analyzed by the Environmental Protection Administration (EPA) strategy to let public understand environmental quality changes in Taiwan, as well as the results of the EPA's laws and policies. The specific work objectives are as follows:

I. Education

From the history of environmental improvement, we raise public awareness of various environmental issues and let pubic work together to protect the environment and protect our environment.

II. Policy advocacy

Let public understand the long-term efforts of government in related fields, as well as the use of taxation and the meaning behind various systems, and the establishment of the reputation of government.

III. Improvement of user experience

User interact with the environmental information can improve user experience. Environmental data used to be hard to use, but after we added infographic and interactive chats and map, it became amazing and easy to understand.
