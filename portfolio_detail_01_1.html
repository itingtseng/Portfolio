Environment Info Push App

The app perfectly integrates 8 different indices to show the real-time environment info along with suggestions for appropriate activities under the various circumstances.

OVERVIEW

About the project

Integrates GPS technology onboard the mobile device to provide the App users with near real time, location based service (LBS) environmental information. The messaging technology is also employed to push subscribed information based on the LBS from Environmental Protection Administration (EPA) server to deliver nearreal time environmental alert message to the users.

  • Role: 
    UI/UX Designer

  • Technology: 
    Sketch, InVision

  • Client: 
    Environmental Protection Administration

  • Year: 
    May 2015-July 2018

GOAL

The Challenge

To face the serious environmental pollution issue, the highest governmental sector, Taiwan EPA has devoted in providing various public services and integrated information around the country for a better living environment. Environment Info Push App was therefore proposed to serve the public with air quality indices, weather info and outdoor activity suggestion.

RESEARCH

The Solution

As EPA is the main and the highest government sector for environment protection that has the authority to access data to nationwide observation stations, all they need was to build a suitable platform for the public to display all the information within one user-friendly and easy-to-use app on Android and iOS devices. I was authorized to develop Environment Info Push App due to my rich experience in providing clients with professional environment-related geospatial solutions.
