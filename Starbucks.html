Starbucks Recycling Support App

Build location-based recycling app that engages, educates, and guidespartners to become sustainability pros. Partners can be a recycling expert for their store and improve how much their store diverts from landfills.

About the project

The Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) graduate program includes a two-semester capstone course in which small teams of HCI master’s degree students complete a user experience design project for a partner company or organization. In a capstone project, an IUPUI team analyzes user and business needs, generates and evaluates design concepts, develops an interactive prototype of the user interface, and collects user feedback on the prototype.
